Escape From Reality

This is a VR game designed in Unity with the theme of escape room puzzle game. While most people design their game around escaping a room, I designed mine to convey the idea of escaping from reality.
The game follows my life as I leave house, take the MRT to school, complete school modules and finally, escape.
I want to remind players that it is okay to want to escape from the reality of their lives sometimes. It is okay to take a break, just do not break yourself.
The actual project consisted of more than just the game, with a written game design document, flow chart, adobe animate renders and storyboard.

This game was made as an assignment in one of my modules and was made in 3 weeks.
No source code can be shown as the file is too big for Github.

Technologies used: C#, Unity, Wave XR, Essence, Online Assets
