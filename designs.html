Hi-Fi SADeaf App

This hi-fi mockup is a prototype for an app that could be made for the Singapore Assosiation for the Deaf (SADeaf).
Of course, as an app developed for people with disabilities, there are accessibility concerns and they are addressed in this app. There is no need for any audio output from the app.
This app can allow users to sign in and out, take courses, practice sign language, edit personal and application settings, quick dial close contacts and show a map to SADeaf. It was made as an assignment in one of my modules and was made in 1 day.

Technologies used: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
